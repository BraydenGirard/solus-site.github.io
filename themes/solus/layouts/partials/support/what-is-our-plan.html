What is our plan?

2017 is dedicated to introducing features and addressing issues, such as:

  1. Dynamic switchable graphics
  2. Integrated systems for recovery, migration, and backup management.
  3. Improving the end-user experience with a major rewrite of Budgie and improvements to our Brisk Menu for MATE.
  4. Enhanced developer tooling.

Alongside these improvements, we are committed to routine releases of Solus. These releases have individual goals by themselves, and you can see more on our Snapshots dashboard.
