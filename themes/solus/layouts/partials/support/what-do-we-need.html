What do we need?

In order to develop Solus, we have to:

  1. Operate a small network of servers
  2. Obtain and replace hardware
  3. Pay for general hardware-related utilities such as network connectivity
  4. Maintain and expand infrastructure like domains and SSL certificates.

Additionally, the enabling of a full-time developer to work on Solus would be beneficial to the execution of our roadmap and many long-term goals the project has.
