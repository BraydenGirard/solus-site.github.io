Desktop for Everyone

Alongside the first-class experience Budgie has under the Solus, we provide official support for Fedora and openSUSE via OBS and work closely with other distribution packagers to enable Budgie to be available on their chosen platforms.

We are continuously improving the Budgie experience in an open, community-driven fashion. We welcome contributions via GitHub. Budgie is supported by the community through the following:

  • budgie-desktop for Arch
  • budgie-desktop-git in Arch User Repository
